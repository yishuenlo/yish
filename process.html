Gamify Habit Building

To make this process more fun and satisfying to track my progress, I've decided to treat it like a RPG game that would include levels, experience points, fighting monsters and quests. Except in this instance, experience points are dollars, monsters are daily practices and daily challenges on Codewar, and quests are specific skillset milestones.

The reward I am aiming for is MSI Stealth Series laptop, due to their power, portability, and slick and professional design. :)

With this system, I am building my habit and skills, and earn the right to use my expensive 'weapon'. And yes, I also intentionally picked a very expensive goal because it'd take me at least 3-4 years to practice coding consistently, so by the time I do receive my reward, I'd be amazing at interactive visualization already (I think... I hope... or maybe just not suck.)
